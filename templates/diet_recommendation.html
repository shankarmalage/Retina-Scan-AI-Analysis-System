🥗 Diabetes Diet Recommendation

✅ Foods to Eat

  • Green Leafy Vegetables
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Fish (Omega-3)
  • Eggs
  • Nuts & Almonds
  • Whole Grains
  • Low-fat Milk
  • Beans & Lentils

❌ Foods to Avoid

  • Soft Drinks
  • Ice Cream
  • White Bread
  • Cakes & Pastries
  • Fast Food
  • Fried Foods
  • Excess Sugar
  • Processed Snacks
  • Alcohol
💡 Tip: Maintaining healthy blood sugar levels can reduce the risk of Diabetic Retinopathy progression and help protect your vision.
